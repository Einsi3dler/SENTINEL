Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sentinel?

Sentinel is an intermediary platform that ensures secure payments and quality assurance for both clients and businesses. Payments are only released after the client approves the work.

How does Sentinel work?

Clients initiate a transaction and make a payment. The payment is held securely by Sentinel until the client reviews and approves the work. Once approved, the payment is released to the service provider.

What types of services does Sentinel support?

Sentinel supports both physical goods (e.g., shoes, bags) and digital services (e.g., website design, graphic design).

How do I sign up for Sentinel?

You can sign up by clicking the "Sign Up Now" button on our homepage and following the registration process.

How secure are the payments on Sentinel?

Payments are held in a secure escrow account and only released to the service provider once the client approves the work, ensuring a safe transaction for both parties.

Can I cancel a transaction?

Yes, transactions can be canceled before the service is completed. Please contact our support team for assistance with cancellations.

What happens if there is a dispute between the client and the service provider?

In case of a dispute, our support team will mediate and help resolve the issue to ensure a fair outcome for both parties.

How long does it take for the service provider to get paid?

Service providers are paid as soon as the client approves the work. The time frame may vary depending on the client's review process.

How can I contact Sentinel support?

You can contact our support team via email at support@sentinel.com or call us at 1-800-123-4567. We are here to assist you with any questions or issues.
